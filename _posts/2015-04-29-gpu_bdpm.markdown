### Bidirectional Photon Mapper
Implemented renderer with Nvidia OptiX. OptiX is a ray-tracing kernel written in CUDA. This renderer was part of a project and a Masters thesis that compares ray batching with other integrators such as Photon mapper and bidirectional path tracer. Here is the [link]() to the repo. This was made by me and [Kaixiang Miao]().
